# 📸 Adding Your Profile Photo

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. Save Your Photo
- Save your profile photo as `profile.jpg` in the `assets/images/` folder
- Replace the existing placeholder file
- Recommended size: 280x280 pixels (square format)
- File format: JPG or PNG
- Keep file size under 200KB for better performance

### 2. Photo Guidelines
For best results, your photo should:
- ✅ Be high quality and well-lit
- ✅ Show your face clearly (head and shoulders)
- ✅ Have a professional appearance
- ✅ Be centered in the frame
- ✅ Have a plain or simple background

### 3. Current Styling Applied
Your photo will automatically be styled with:
- 🎨 **Circular shape** with professional border
- 🌟 **Hover effects** (slight scale and glow)
- 📱 **Responsive sizing** for all devices
- ⚡ **Smooth animations** and transitions
- 🎯 **Perfect centering** and alignment

### 4. Responsive Behavior
- **Desktop**: 280px × 280px
- **Tablet**: 200px × 200px  
- **Mobile**: 180px × 180px

### 5. What's Already Set Up
The portfolio is configured to:
- Load your photo immediately (high priority)
- Display it as a perfect circle
- Add a beautiful gradient overlay
- Include hover animations
- Optimize for all screen sizes

### 6. Quick Test
After adding your photo:
1. Refresh your browser at `http://localhost:8000`
2. Check that your photo appears correctly
3. Test hover effects by moving your mouse over it
4. Verify mobile responsiveness
